About us

It all begins here...

The inception of the Sri Lankan Airlines Discord server is a tale of digital community building and aviation enthusiasm. It began with a visionary founder, That one Boeing pilot with a passion for connecting people who share a love for the skies. He would then lay the foundation for a virtual space where aviation enthusiasts could gather, share experiences, and discuss their shared interest in Sri Lankan Airlines. The server gained momentum when Primera, the founder of the Spirit Airlines Discord server, joined as its first human member. Primera brought with them a wealth of experience in cultivating online communities, having already established a popular server for Spirit Airlines enthusiasts. Together, these pioneers created a hub for discussion, support, and camaraderie among fans of aviation, setting the stage for a thriving community that continues to welcome members from all over the world. Then Sri Lankan got its first CEO Yaz and its first partner, The indie alliance The server's growth is a testament to the power of shared interests and the bonds that can form even in the vast expanse of cyberspace.
